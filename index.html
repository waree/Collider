How to play Collider

Keep your health up and get points by collecting at least 3 balls in a row or in a column.

Shoot balls from the inner borders! (It must collide with another in the board.)



You can move a ball while it's normal sized or it can collide with another in the board.



After moving it shrinks and become smaller and unmovable.



By collecting rows and columns you gain combos. Collect 3 combos in the same colour to activate their effects.

Special effects of the colours:
3 red: +10% life
3 blue: clear all blue balls
3 yellow: transform all the outer border balls to yellow
3 green: all of the balls movable again
