How to play

Collect at least 3 balls in a row or in a column.

You can't shoot a ball from the borders if it can't collide with another in the board.



You can move a ball while it can collide with another in the board.



After moving it shrinks and become unmovable.



Combos:
3 red: +10% life
3 blue: explode all blue balls
3 yellow: transform all outer balls to yellow
3 green: all of the balls movable again
